The Reiter laboratory values excellence, persistance, diversity and curiosity. Facilities within the laboratory, the Department of Neurology, the Neuroscience Institute and the campus include for fluorescence and confocal microscopy, neural tracing, cell culture and fly genetics. We also have access to outstanding core facilities on campus including sequencing, bioinformatics, mass-spectrometry, and FACS sorting at the University of Tennessee Health Science Center.

 

Interested in a postdoctoral position in the Reiter Lab?

We’re hiring! Send a cover letter explaining your research experience and future interests to Larry Reiter at lreiter at uthsc.edu. Include your current CV and contact information for three references.

 

Would you like to be a graduate student in the Reiter Lab?

We take graduate students through the Neuroscience Track in the Integrated Biomedical Sciences Program (IBSP) in the College of Graduate Education at UTHSC. You can learn more about applying to the program at https://www.uthsc.edu/graduate-health-sciences/ibs/research-tracks/index.php.
