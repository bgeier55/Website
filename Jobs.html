The Markstein laboratory values excellence, innovation, collegiality, and diversity. We have state-of-the-art facilities within our laboratory for fluorescence and confocal microscopy, chemical screening, molecular biology, and fly genetics. We also have superb core facilities on campus for sequencing, mass-spectrometry, and imaging at the UMass Institute for Applied Life Sciences.

Interested in a postdoctoral position?

We’re hiring! Send a cover letter explaining your current and future research interests to Michele Markstein at mmarkstein@bio.umass.edu. Include your CV and contact information for three references.

Curious about graduate student positions?

We take graduate students through the Molecular and Cellular Biology (MCB) Graduate Program. You can learn more about the program and send your application at https://www.bio.umass.edu/mcb. If you already have experience working with Drosophila, send a cover letter explaining your research interests to Michele Markstein at mmarkstein@bio.umass.edu.
